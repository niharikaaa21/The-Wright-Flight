View Bookings :

Easily access your booked flights and manage your travel plans in one place. Simply log in to your account and navigate to the "My Bookings" section. Here, you'll find details of your upcoming and past flights, including flight numbers, dates, and destinations.

Logout :

Protect your account and personal information by logging out securely when you're done. Simply click on your profile icon or account settings, then select "Log Out." Confirm your choice, and you'll be safely signed out of your account. Whether you're using a desktop or mobile device, logging out ensures your privacy and security until your next visit.
